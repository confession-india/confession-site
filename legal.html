Legal & Privacy

1. Absolute Anonymity

Confess India is built on the principle of secrecy. We do not store IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or precise locations. Your confessions are linked only to a random ID generated by the system.

2. Data Usage

Confessions are stored in a secure Firebase database. We do not share this data with third parties. Once a post is deleted by the admin or the user session, it is purged from our active records.

3. Content Moderation

We use an automated multilingual safety filter. Posts containing illegal threats, severe abuse, or private personal information (doxing) will be automatically flagged and removed by the system.
